About Our Application

Our application is a comprehensive solution for skin cancer detection and patient care, akin to property management for healthcare. It employs advanced technology, including Convolutional Neural Networks, to accurately diagnose skin conditions, streamlines patient-physician interactions, and provides utilities for billing and communication. Much like a rental application assesses tenant suitability and property maintenance, our application is dedicated to improving patient outcomes and contributing to the fight against skin cancer by ensuring timely and accurate care.
