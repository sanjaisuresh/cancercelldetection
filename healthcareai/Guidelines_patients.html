Guidelines for Using Our Website Wisely

  1. Secure Your Account:
    • Ensure your login credentials are kept confidential.
    • Use a strong and unique password.
    • Enable two-factor authentication if available.
  2. Respect Privacy:
    • Do not share personal or sensitive information on public forums.
    • Respect the privacy of other users.
  3. Use Responsibly:
    • Use the platform for its intended purposes, such as tracking your health or communicating with healthcare providers.
    • Avoid any misuse, harassment, or illegal activities.
  4. Report Issues:
    • Report any technical issues or suspicious activities to our support team.
  5. Stay Informed:
    • Read and follow the terms of service and privacy policy.
  6. Be Mindful of Health Data:
    • Share accurate health information with healthcare professionals.
    • Do not self-diagnose or make treatment decisions solely based on online information.
  7. Regularly Update:
    • Keep your profile and health information up to date.
  8. Engage Respectfully:
    • Communicate respectfully with healthcare providers and fellow patients.
  9. Billing and Payments:
    • Follow billing procedures as outlined in the platform.
    • Report any billing discrepancies.
  10. Community Guidelines:
    • If applicable, adhere to the community guidelines in discussion forums or groups.
